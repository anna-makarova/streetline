Zoner = Real Estate Full Package – The One and Only!

Zoner is Real Estate HTML template fully loaded with awesome features. Zoner brings the OpenStreetMap support as the first template on ThemeForest! You get also styled Google Maps as well, so you can decided which map provider you want to choose.

Another awesome feature is the Parallax Map Scrolling. Also as the first template on TF Zoner brings modern parallax effect for the map. Impress your visitors and make your Real Estate portal more unique!

Do you like Masonry effect on blog sites? Good for you! Zoner gives you this awesome effect to the property listing as well! Another point to make your website looking great.

You can also choose what markers and icons do you like. There are several property type icons included in the package, but there is no limit to use your own. The whole template is made with excellent responsiveness - you should definitely check the Zoner on mobile devices.
